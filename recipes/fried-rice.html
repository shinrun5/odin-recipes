Description

I love fried rice
cuz chinese
Use some vegies and lots of oil (don't try any of that healthy shi here)

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • More Rice
  • Even more Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • msg/salt
  • frozen veggies
  • egg
  • Protein of choice

Steps

  1. Have some old, cold rice that have been sitting inside the fridge for a few days
  2. Prepare your ingredients
  3. Heat up your wok with shit ton of oil
  4. Vivet your protein(if any) and also cook your egg
  5. stir fry eggs, garlic/ginger and your veggies
  6. Toss in rice and stir cook for a while
  7. Toss in sauce and msg/salt
  8. Finish with green onion on top
